# 24 Climate Investment Concepts

**Stanford GSB GEN 390 — Unlocking HNWI Climate Capital**

---

### Financial Architecture

1. **Climate Catalyst Fund** — First-loss capital that de-risks private investment in Series B/C climate companies, the underfunded "messy middle" between seed-stage VCs and infrastructure-scale project finance.

2. **Climate Option Fund** — Invests in climate solutions we hope we'll never need, pricing the option value of having them ready if we do.

3. **Perpetual Climate Holding Company** — An investment vehicle designed for century-scale climate ownership, rejecting the 7-year fund model entirely. Permanent capital. No forced exits.

4. **Climate FOMO Fund** — A climate investment vehicle where the primary value proposition is exclusivity and social proof, explicitly leveraging status anxiety to move capital.

5. **Redesigning the Carry** — A new incentive architecture so that CIOs and investment advisors are rewarded for patience rather than punished for it. Vintage-weighted performance, governance bridges, reputation shields.

6. **Ecosystem Stake** — Buy shares in a specific ecosystem (forest, wetland, reef, watershed) where value appreciates as the ecosystem recovers.

### Investor Infrastructure

7. **Shared Diligence Syndicate** — Family offices pool their climate deal due diligence, turning research from competitive advantage into collective infrastructure.

8. **CIO Conversion Program** — An immersive experience that transforms traditional CIOs into climate-integrated investors, working on the change agent, not the principal.

9. **Climate Risk as Wealth Preservation** — Makes the climate risk already embedded in family portfolios visible, then connects that visibility directly to investable responses.

### Risk & Insurance

10. **Resilience Guarantee Company** — Wraps performance guarantees around nature-based solutions, making them as bankable as concrete. "This wetland will reduce flood damage by 40%. If it doesn't, we pay."

11. **Supply Chain Climate Insurer** — Protects businesses against climate disruption to their supply chains, and invests premiums in fixing the underlying risks.

12. **Climate Underwriter** — An AI platform that predicts climate risk for any asset, anywhere, at any time horizon.

13. **Adaptation Marketplace** — Convenes adaptation solutions around specific climate perils, bundles them into packages that make regions insurable again, funded by family offices whose own assets depend on it.

### Nature & Ecosystems

14. **Natural Capital Partners** — Helps corporations invest in the natural systems their businesses depend on. Starts with dependency mapping: "Your cotton comes from regions facing 40% water stress increase by 2035."

15. **Nature's Board Seat** — A legal and governance structure that gives ecosystems formal voting representation in corporate decisions.

16. **Supply Chain Resilience Syndicate** — Convenes competing companies to jointly invest in the ecosystems their supply chains depend on, making resilience a shared infrastructure cost.

### Water & Cooling

17. **Water Abundance Company** — Sells long-term water security to cities, farms, and corporations, and invests in everything that creates more water.

18. **AquaSense** — Deploys soil sensors and AI irrigation recommendations to smallholder farmers, monetized through water utilities and agricultural lenders who benefit when farmers use less water.

19. **Cooling Utility** — Sells cool spaces, not air conditioners, using whatever mix of solutions works best. Outcome contracts: "We guarantee 72F for $X/month."

### Climate Intelligence

20. **Climate Compass** — Models future livability at the hyperlocal level and provides financial products enabling proactive relocation before climate disasters strike.

### Transition & Workforce

21. **Transition Workforce Company** — A staffing company that employs fossil fuel workers and deploys them to clean energy projects. Just transition as a product, not a slogan.

22. **Transition Corps** — A national service program that employs young people in climate work for 2 years, building the next generation of climate workforce.

23. **Transition Policy Shop** — Helps climate businesses navigate policy: lobbying, regulatory strategy, and coalition building as a service.

### Collective Action

24. **Big Bet Factory** — Creates pre-competitive climate coalitions, bringing together competitors to fund solutions too big for any single player.
